First Generation

Extra-Osseous TaloTarsal Stabilization

HyProCure® I

The original HyProCure titanium stent is placed into the sinus tarsi — the naturally occurring space between the ankle bone and heel bone — without cutting, drilling, or permanently altering the bone. It corrects the root cause of misalignment with a reversible, minimally invasive procedure.

Titanium sinus tarsi stent — no cutting or drilling required
Surgery takes less than 30 minutes; immediate weight-bearing in post-op shoe
Full return to activity in approximately 2 weeks
Reversible — only 5.2% removal rate vs. up to 58% for competitors
100,000+ implants placed worldwide since 2004

Next-Generation Stabilization

HyProCure® II
