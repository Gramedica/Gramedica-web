Step-by-step: Outlook signature editor

1
Click Select My Signature on your card below — your signature content will highlight in blue
2
Press Ctrl + C on your keyboard to copy it
3
In Outlook, click ⚙️ gear icon (top right) → Accounts → Signatures
4
Click the pencil ✏️ next to your signature name to open the editor
5
Click inside the editor, press Ctrl+A to select everything, then press Delete
6
Press Ctrl+V to paste your new signature, then click Save
7
Make sure both boxes are ticked: "Set default for new messages" and "Set default for replies and forwards"
