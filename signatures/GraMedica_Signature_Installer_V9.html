⚠ Important: Paste into the signature editor only — via ⚙ → Accounts → Signatures → ✎ pencil. Do not paste directly into an email body.

Step-by-step: Outlook signature editor

1
Click Select My Signature — it highlights in blue
2
Press Ctrl + C to copy
3
In Outlook click ⚙ gear (top right) → Accounts → Signatures
4
Click ✎ pencil next to your signature name
5
Click inside the editor → Ctrl+A → Delete
6
Press Ctrl+V to paste → click Save
7
Tick both: "Set default for new messages" and "Set default for replies and forwards"
